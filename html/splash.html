Science UP

"SUP"

Collaborative datasharing between research mentors and students

Login/Register

Check In

Using the Yo platform, students are able to update their mentors when they reach the lab simply with the click of a button.

Message

Messaging offers simple communication between mentors and students.Students and mentors are able to use messaging to discuss research topics, plan for experiments, and ask/answer questions.

Upload

The upload feature allows mentors and students to share videos, photos, research articles, and other documents.
